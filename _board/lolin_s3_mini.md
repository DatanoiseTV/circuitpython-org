WiFi & Bluetooth 5 (LE) development boards based ESP32-S3FH4R2.

## Features

- based ESP32-S3FH4R2
- Type-C USB
- 4MB Flash
- 2MB PSRAM
- Clock speed: 240 Mhz
- Digital I/O Pins: 27
- ADC, DAC, I2C, SPI, UART, USB OTG
- RGB LED (IO47)
- Size: 34.3 mm x 25.4 mm
- Weight: 3g
- Default firmware: Micropython
